Pokémon Infinite Fusion

Pokémon Infinite Fusion is a Pokémon rom hack that re-introduces the DNA Splicers from Black and White, allowing you to combine any two Pokémon into a new one. It was inspired by the Pokémon fusion generator.

There is a large community effort to give unique sprites to every fusion. You can see the sprites that have been made on the Fusiondex. Anyone is free to contribute through joining the project's discord. I recommend it, it's fun! Below are some sprites I've made and submitted:
