I first played Petz 5 as a kid after ordering it out of a Scholastic book fair magazine. I quickly discovered the online hexing community, and I've loved the games ever since.

I didn't make a website of my own until 2020, when I got back into Half-Life (another important game of my childhood) and decided to try and recreate the aliens from it in Petz. This led to a website called New Lambda... which has turned into the site you currently browse!

This site has expanded beyond Petz, but it wouldn't be here if my interest in the games hadn't been rekindled. Please enjoy what I've made! :)


If you are looking for my Half-Life hexes, please check out the Research Facility.
